## Key Features

### Facebook Account Warm-Up
MadHub’s **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature simulates real user behavior to enhance account activity and reduce the risk of account bans. This feature intelligently navigates through homepage feeds and video pages, skips ad videos, and allows for interaction through likes and comments. You can set interaction probability and execution distribution to customize how your account behaves, making it appear more authentic to the Facebook algorithm.

**Capabilities**:
- Simulate organic browsing behavior to enhance account trust.
- Skip ads to focus on genuine content engagement.
- Configure interaction settings to match your marketing strategy.

### Usage Instructions
To utilize the **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature, follow these simple steps:
1. Launch MadHub on your Android device, cloud phone, or emulator.
2. Navigate to the **Facebook Account Warm-Up** section in the interface.
3. Select the account you want to warm up.
4. Set your desired interaction probability (e.g., 70% like, 30% comment).
5. Choose your execution probability distribution based on your goals.

### Configuration Example
Here’s how to configure the settings effectively:
- **Interaction Probability**: Set to 70% for likes and 30% for comments.
- **Execution Frequency**: Choose a frequency that suits your engagement strategy, such as every 10 minutes.
- **Loop Mode**: Enable loop mode to continuously perform warm-up actions throughout the day without manual intervention.

This feature is particularly suitable for new account nurturing and old account maintenance, allowing you to establish a trustworthy profile or bolster the credibility of longstanding accounts.

### Facebook Group Auto-Posting
Another robust feature is MadHub's **Facebook Group Auto-Posting**. This allows you to automatically post content across multiple groups you have joined, significantly enhancing your marketing reach and engagement without the need for repetitive manual posting.

**Capabilities**:
- Supports both input and file modes for content submission.
- Allows content rotation and loop posting to maximize visibility.
- Set specific post counts for individual groups and total posts.

### Usage Instructions
To set up the **Facebook Group Auto-Posting** feature:
1. Access the **Group Auto-Posting** feature from the main menu.
2. Select the groups where you want to post.
3. Upload your content file or enter your post directly.
4. Configure the rotation settings to keep your content fresh.
5. Set the total post count and specify how many times to post in each group.

### Configuration Example
For effective configuration, consider the following parameters:
- **Content Input Mode**: Upload a CSV file with various posts.
- **Number of Posts per Group**: Set to 5 to ensure each group receives consistent engagement.
- **Post Visibility**: Choose to post publicly or to group members only based on your marketing goals.

This feature is ideal for marketers looking to expand their reach within Facebook groups, engage with community members, and promote their products or services effectively.
